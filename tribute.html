LECRAE - Christian Rapper Extraordinaire

Lecrae Devaughn Moore popularly known as Lecrae, is an American Christian rapper, singer, songwriter, record and film producer, record executive, actor, and entrepreneur.

Todays tribute is an award winning rapper that is not shy to proclaim the word of God and the wonders of serving Jesus through music. Lecrae's works has served as a source of hope and inspiration to youths that holiness and standing up for the faith in Jesus is indeed a path that evolves in greatness and success.

Let's journey back in time to the unveiling of a star.

10 things you did not know about Lecrae

  • Lecrae Devaughn Moore was born on the 9th of October 9, 1979
  • Lecrae never met his father until 2020 and was raised by a single mother in the cityn of Houston, Texas, United States of America.
  • Lecrae says that it was at age 19 that he finally decided to live for God, though "it wasn't overnight" and he "spent a lot of time making bad decisions."
  • In 2004, five years after his conversion, Lecrae teamed up with Ben Washer to found Reach Records, and at the age of 25 he released his first album, Real Talk.
  • After the success of Real Talk, Lecrae then released his second studio album on August 15, 2006. After the Music Stops charted at No. 5 on the Billboard Gospel Albums chart, No. 7 on the Billboard Christian Albums chart and No. 16 on the Billboard Heatseeker Album charts, and received a nomination for a Dove Award, as was the single "Jesus Muzik", featuring Trip Lee.
  • In 2007, 116 Clique released its second album, 13 Letters, it reached No. 10 on the Gospel Albums chart and No. 29 on the Christian Albums chart. 116 Clique also released the remix EP Amped, which peaked at No. 24 on the Gospel Albums chart.
  • Between 2008 to 2011, Lecrae released two albums "Rebel" and "Rebel Reloaded". He also got his first film role in the Uprise Presents: Word from the Street by the UK-based TV channel OHTV.
  • 2012-2016 will see this star ... rise like a city set upon a hill [Matthew 5:14b]. Lecrae will go on to release the album Gravity which debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200, with 72,000 units sold. The album also debuted at No. 1 on the Christian, Gospel, Independent, and Rap Album charts, No. 3 on the Digital Albums chart, and 24 on the Canadian Albums Chart. After the iTunes deluxe version of the album hit No. 1 on that vendors charts, and the regular version at number No. 2, Time wrote an article about Lecrae and his success with the album.
  • Lecrae will go on to release 3 more mixtapes and his seventh album called Anomaly which will be the first album to be no.1 on the Billboard 200 and the Gospel Charts. His mixtapes also featured on these charts with some topping other charts too.
